Who We Are

Sombreiro Energy Limited was incorporated in 2018 and has since grown into a diversified energy company specializing in:

  • Engineering, Procurement, & Construction (EPC): Delivering high-quality engineering solutions across petrochemical, oil and gas, water, and power industries.
  • Renewable Energy Solutions: Designing, marketing, and installing solar power systems for homes, businesses, and industries, ensuring clean and sustainable energy access.
  • Energy Trading: Sourcing and marketing refined petroleum products and liquefied natural gas (LNG) to meet the growing energy demands of businesses and industries.
