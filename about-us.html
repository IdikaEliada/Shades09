Welcome to Sombreiro Energy Limited

As a Leading Renewable Energy Solutions Providing Company in Nigeria Empowering both Urban and Rural Communities to harness the Power of the Sun in generating Electricity, our mission is to bridge the Energy gap in the Country by providing Clean, Reliable, Sustainable and Affordable Energy Solutions for our clients Homes, Businesses and Communities.

Who We Are

Sombreiro Energy Limited is a leading Nigerian energy solutions provider, incorporated in 2018. We are dedicated to delivering innovative, sustainable, and cost-effective energy services to businesses, communities, and educational institutions across Nigeria and West Africa.

Our team is made up of experienced engineers, project managers, and renewable energy specialists who are passionate about driving the transition to clean energy. We pride ourselves on our technical expertise, customer-centric approach, and commitment to excellence.

  • Engineering, Procurement, & Construction (EPC): We deliver high-quality engineering solutions for petrochemical, oil & gas, water, and power industries.
  • Renewable Energy Solutions: We design, market, and install solar power systems for homes, businesses, and schools, ensuring clean and reliable energy access.
  • Energy Trading: We source and market refined petroleum products and liquefied natural gas (LNG) to meet the growing energy demands of our clients.
  • Supporting Schools: We empower educational institutions with reliable solar power, energy efficiency audits, and training for staff and students.
